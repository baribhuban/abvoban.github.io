Hi! I am a Postgraduate student in Artificial Intelligence at Nanjing University of Information Science and Technology (NUIST), Nanjing, China. Supervised by Jiang Hao. Previously, I received my Bsc. in Computer Science and Technology from NUIST

Research

I build efficient, adaptable computer vision systems for medical imaging and remote sensing. domains where labeled data is scarce and imaging conditions constantly shift across scanners, modalities, and geographies.My work follows two directions: designing data-adaptive architectures for semantic segmentation, deformable attention for object detectionDANet'23 and transformer-based deep fusion for confocal microendoscopy segmentation UperNet-ViT, and adapting vision foundation models for specialized domains under few-shot and limited-supervision settings, including frequency-guided Mamba decoding FreCaM and DINOv3-based multi-scale segmentation UNet-MSMamba for remote sensing. MRI-based bone age prediction, and instance segmentation for autonomous drivingICCIT'25
